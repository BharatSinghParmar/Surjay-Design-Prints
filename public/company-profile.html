Company Overview

A Textile Manufacturing
Partner Built on Process.

Surjay Design & Prints is a B2B textile manufacturing company based in Rajasthan, India. The company specializes in fabric dyeing, screen printing, hand printing, and textile finishing for wholesalers, garment manufacturers, and bulk buyers.

Every meter of fabric processed at our facility follows a structured manufacturing workflow — from raw fabric procurement through dyeing, printing, pressing, quality inspection, and packaging — designed to deliver consistent, market-ready output.

Quality Assurance

Process Discipline at Every Stage.

Quality is not a final check — it is embedded in every stage of our manufacturing process, from raw material selection to packaging.

01

Raw Material Inspection

Every fabric lot is inspected for construction, weight, and suitability before entering the manufacturing process.

02

Color Consistency Testing

Shade matching and uniformity checks are conducted across the batch to maintain visual consistency.

03

Fabric Surface Inspection

Each lot is examined for weaving defects, print registration accuracy, and finish quality.

04

Dimensional Stability

Width, elongation, and shrinkage parameters are monitored during pressing and finishing.

05

Packing Standards

Final folding, packaging and labelling follow standard procedures for market-ready dispatch.

✓

Quality Commitment

Our manufacturing process is designed to ensure consistency, quality, and reliability at every stage of production.
